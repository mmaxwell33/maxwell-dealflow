How the numbers happen

It's not luck. It's preparation.

Behind every closed deal is the same three-part discipline. Nothing flashy — just consistent application.

🎯

Price it right the first time

Sellers who overprice spend more time on market and end up selling for less. Buyers who overpay never get that money back. I price with the comps, not with hope.

📸

Present it properly

Professional photography. Honest listing copy. The right showings cadence. Most "stale" listings just had weak presentation — fix that and the market responds.

🤝

Negotiate without ego

The goal is your goal — getting you to closing on terms that work. I push hard when it helps, fold when it doesn't, and never let pride cost you a deal.
