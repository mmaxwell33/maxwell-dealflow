About

Early in my career. Serious about every deal.

REALTOR® · eXp Realty · St. John's, NL

I'm Maxwell Delali Midodzi, a REALTOR® serving St. John's and the Avalon Peninsula. I'm newer to the industry — but that's exactly why every client gets my full attention. I'm not coasting on a Rolodex; I'm building a practice the right way, one careful deal at a time.

My approach is simple: be honest, do the homework, and treat every client like a long-term relationship — not a transaction. I'd rather lose a deal than push someone into the wrong house. The agents I most respect built their reputations the same way.

My approach

Less theatre. More homework.

Three principles I refuse to compromise on, no matter how much the market changes.

🔍

Do the homework first

Before any showing, I pull the comps, the days-on-market history, and the neighbourhood trend lines. You'll walk in knowing what the place is actually worth.

🗣️

Tell you what I see

If the foundation looks suspect, I'll say so. If the asking price is fair, I'll say so. Real estate is too expensive for polite half-truths.

📱

Stay in touch

You'll hear from me by your preferred method — text, email, phone, or in person. No "ghosting" after the listing is signed. Weekly updates, minimum.
