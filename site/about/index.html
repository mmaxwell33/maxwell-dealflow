Meet Maxwell

REALTOR® · eXp Realty

I'm a REALTOR® with eXp Realty, working across St. John's and the Avalon Peninsula. I'm newer to real estate, and I work like it matters. I read the whole inspection report. I do the homework before the showing. And I give every client the same care, whether it's your first home or one you've lived in for thirty years.

I also built my own tools to keep your deal organized. A lot of agents run their pipeline on sticky notes and hope nothing slips. I built a system that tracks the bookings, offers, deadlines, and documents for me, so nothing gets lost. You get a private link to watch your deal progress in real time, and your booking confirmations arrive automatically. You won't have to chase me for an update.

I work across St. John's, Mount Pearl, Paradise, Conception Bay South, Torbay, and the smaller communities around the Avalon. I don't recommend a neighbourhood off a map. I recommend one because I've walked it and done the research on what a fair price looks like there.

Whether you're months from your first showing or a year out from listing, I'm happy to talk through the market, the process, or what's realistic for you. No pressure.

My approach

Less theatre. More homework.

Three things I stick to.

🔍

Do the homework first

Before any showing, I pull the comps, the days-on-market history, and the neighbourhood trend lines. You'll walk in knowing what the place is actually worth.

🗣️

Tell you what I see

If the foundation looks suspect, I'll say so. If the asking price is fair, I'll say so. Real estate is too expensive for polite half-truths.

📱

Stay in touch

You'll hear from me by whatever method you prefer: text, email, phone, or in person. I don't go quiet after the listing is signed. Weekly updates at least.
