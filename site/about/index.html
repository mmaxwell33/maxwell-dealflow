About Maxwell

The agent your friends keep recommending.

REALTOR® · eXp Realty · St. John's, NL

I'm Maxwell Delali Midodzi, a REALTOR® serving St. John's and the Avalon Peninsula. I help first-time buyers find homes they actually love, growing families upgrade without losing their minds, and sellers price + market their properties for the best possible outcome.

My approach is simple: be honest, do the homework, and treat every client like a long-term relationship — not a transaction. Most of my business comes from referrals, which means I'd rather lose a deal than push someone into the wrong house.

My approach

Less theatre. More homework.

Three principles I refuse to compromise on, no matter how much the market changes.

🔍

Do the homework first

Before any showing, I pull the comps, the days-on-market history, and the neighbourhood trend lines. You'll walk in knowing what the place is actually worth.

🗣️

Tell you what I see

If the foundation looks suspect, I'll say so. If the asking price is fair, I'll say so. Real estate is too expensive for polite half-truths.

📱

Stay in touch

You'll hear from me by your preferred method — text, email, phone, or in person. No "ghosting" after the listing is signed. Weekly updates, minimum.

What I specialise in

Newfoundland-grade real estate, end-to-end.

Whether you're new to the Avalon or have lived here your whole life, here's what I can help with.

🏠

First-time buyers

The mortgage paperwork, the inspection report, the negotiation back-and-forth — I'll walk you through every step so it's never the first time you've heard the word.

  • Mortgage pre-approval guidance
  • School and neighbourhood matching
  • Inspection coordination
  • Closing paperwork end-to-end
🏗️

New builds

Buying off a plan or mid-construction is its own game. I work directly with local builders and track every milestone so you're never the last to know.

  • Builder reputation and contract review
  • Lot selection and floor-plan trade-offs
  • Construction-progress monitoring
  • Walkthrough and deficiency lists
🏷

Sellers

I'll value your home using current Avalon comparables, prep the listing the right way, and market it where actual buyers look — not just on MLS and hope.

  • Comparable market analysis (CMA)
  • Pre-listing prep and staging advice
  • Professional photography
  • MLS + social + partner-network marketing
🔄

Move-up & relocation

Selling your current place and buying the next one at the same time is a coordination puzzle. I run both sides so the timeline doesn't trap you between two mortgages.

  • Coordinated buy + sell timelines
  • Bridge financing guidance
  • Out-of-province relocations
  • Rental-to-ownership transitions
