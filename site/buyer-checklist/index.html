First-time home buyer checklist, Newfoundland.

A working checklist for buying your first home in St. John's or the Avalon. Tick items as you complete them. Print this page for a paper copy. Every step here happens in the same order, give or take a week.

Phase 1

Before you start looking

2 to 4 weeks before any showings

Phase 2

Get pre-approved

1 to 3 days · talk to one or more lenders

Phase 3

Search and showings

2 weeks to 6 months, depending on inventory + criteria

Phase 4

Make an offer

1 to 3 days from "let's offer" to a signed agreement

Phase 5

Conditions period (due diligence)

5 to 14 days from accepted offer

Between firm sale and closing

3 to 8 weeks of mostly waiting
