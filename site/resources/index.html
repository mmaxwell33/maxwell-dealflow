Resources

Useful links for Newfoundland buyers and sellers.

A working reference of government programs, calculators, associations, and tools I find most useful when working with clients. Bookmark this page — most of these links you'll come back to.

Note: These are public resources from organizations I'm not affiliated with. I'm not earning anything from listing them here. Always verify current details (rates, eligibility, deadlines) directly on the linked site — government programs change.

Mortgage and financing

Start here if you're trying to understand what you can afford, how much you'll pay monthly, or which government programs apply.

  • FCAC mortgage calculator itools-ioutils.fcac-acfc.gc.ca

    Government of Canada's official mortgage calculator. Run amortization scenarios, see total interest paid, model different payment frequencies. Free, no signup, no email harvested.

    →
  • CMHC mortgage calculator cmhc-schl.gc.ca

    Canada Mortgage and Housing Corporation's calculator — particularly useful for figuring out the CMHC insurance premium you'll pay on a down payment below 20%.

    →
  • First-Time Home Buyers' Tax Credit (HBTC) canada.ca

    A non-refundable federal tax credit (currently up to $1,500) for eligible first-time buyers. Worth claiming on your tax return after closing.

    →
  • Home Buyers' Plan (RRSP withdrawal) canada.ca

    Lets first-time buyers withdraw up to a set amount from their RRSP tax-free toward a down payment, repaid over 15 years. Check the current limit on the CRA page.

    →
  • First Home Savings Account (FHSA) canada.ca

    A registered savings account combining the best features of an RRSP and a TFSA, designed specifically for first-home purchases. Contributions are tax-deductible; withdrawals for a first home are tax-free.

    →

Professional associations and verification

Want to verify a REALTOR®, inspector, or lawyer is properly licensed? These are the official registries.

  • Newfoundland & Labrador Association of REALTORS® nlar.ca

    The provincial REALTOR® association. Verify any agent is in good standing and has the registration they claim.

    →
  • Canadian Real Estate Association (CREA) crea.ca

    The national body. Operates Realtor.ca and sets the REALTOR® code of ethics. Their consumer-facing site has market reports and buyer/seller guides.

    →
  • Realtor.ca realtor.ca

    The public MLS portal. Every active listing in Canada appears here within 24 hours of going on market. Free to search, no signup required.

    →
  • Canadian Association of Home and Property Inspectors (CAHPI) cahpi.ca

    Find a certified home inspector. CAHPI members follow a standardized inspection methodology and carry errors-and-omissions insurance.

    →
  • Law Society of Newfoundland & Labrador lawsociety.nl.ca

    Find a real-estate lawyer or verify one is in good standing. Their "Find a Lawyer" tool filters by practice area and location.

    →

Provincial and municipal

Property tax records, municipal services, and provincial real-estate consumer protection.

  • City of St. John's stjohns.ca

    Property tax info, garbage and recycling schedules, permits, zoning bylaws. Useful when comparing two properties in different parts of the city.

    →
  • City of Mount Pearl mountpearl.ca

    Municipal services for Mount Pearl. Property tax estimator, permit applications, school catchment info.

    →
  • Town of Paradise townofparadise.ca

    Municipal site for Paradise. Property tax, services, council news, planning bylaws.

    →
  • Town of Conception Bay South conceptionbaysouth.ca

    Municipal site for CBS. Useful for property tax rates and zoning info before making an offer in CBS.

    →
  • NL Digital Government and Service NL gov.nl.ca

    Where you'd register a property change, look up real-estate license verification, or pull provincial title-related records.

    →

Utilities and moving

Set these up before closing day. Most utility transfers need 2–5 business days' notice.

  • NL Hydro nlhydro.com

    Electricity service for most of NL outside of urban St. John's / Mount Pearl. Transfer or new-account requests are online.

    →
  • Newfoundland Power newfoundlandpower.com

    Electricity distribution in the urban St. John's metro and Avalon. New-customer and move-day requests.

    →
  • Canada Post mail forwarding canadapost-postescanada.ca

    Forward your mail to your new address for up to a year. Set up 7–10 business days before closing.

    →

Reading and education

Solid plain-language guides to specific topics you might want to dig into.

  • CMHC: Buying your first home cmhc-schl.gc.ca

    Government's free guide to first-time home buying. Covers savings, financing, choosing a property, and closing.

    →
  • FCAC: Mortgage basics canada.ca

    The Financial Consumer Agency of Canada's plain-language explainers on mortgages, rates, term vs amortization, and renewal.

    →
  • CREA market statistics crea.ca

    National and provincial real-estate market trends — monthly sales, price index, time on market. Worth checking before deciding to list.

    →
