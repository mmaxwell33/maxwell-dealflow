🚀 Agent Portal Deployment
Deploy a new CRM portal for another agent. Each agent gets their own isolated account — they cannot see your data, clients, or transactions.
ℹ️ How It Works
  • A new agent account is created in the system
  • The agent gets their own login with their own data
  • Their data is completely isolated — you don't see theirs, they don't see yours
  • A welcome email is sent to the agent with login instructions
📖 Agent Portal Guide
▶

Step 1: Fill in the agent's full name and email below.

Step 2: Click "Deploy Agent Portal" — a Supabase Auth invite will be sent to their email.

Step 3: The agent clicks the invite link, sets their password, and logs in.

Step 4: Their account is live — completely isolated from yours.

⚠️ Make sure the email is correct before deploying. Invites cannot be recalled.
